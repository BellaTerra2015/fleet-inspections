🔧 Unit 102 Bowie Hydroseeder

Grease Point Reference Guide | Kenworth W900B (1989)

⚠️ Lubrication Critical for Equipment Life

The Bowie hydroseeder has multiple grease points that must be serviced regularly. This guide shows the exact location of each zerk fitting to ensure proper maintenance and prevent costly breakdowns.

📋 Greasing Schedule

Component Frequency Grease Type
Front Zerk Row (6 points) Every 50 hours or weekly Multi-purpose NLGI #2
Back Zerk Row (6 points) Every 50 hours or weekly Multi-purpose NLGI #2
MCM Pump Bearing Every 100 hours or monthly Multi-purpose NLGI #2

🔧 Greasing Procedure

  • Clean zerks first: Wipe each zerk fitting clean before greasing to prevent contamination
  • Use grease gun: Attach grease gun securely to each zerk
  • Pump until resistance: Add grease until you feel resistance (old grease purging out)
  • Don't over-grease: Stop when grease begins to seep from seals
  • Check for leaks: Wipe excess grease and check for damaged fittings
  • Log maintenance: Record date and hours in maintenance log

⚠️ Important Notes

  • Yellow caps on zerks indicate marine-grade or high-pressure fittings
  • Missing or damaged caps should be replaced immediately to prevent dirt intrusion
  • If zerk won't accept grease, check for blockage or damaged fitting
  • Greasing is especially critical before cold weather operation
  • Document all maintenance in BusyBusy or shop maintenance log
